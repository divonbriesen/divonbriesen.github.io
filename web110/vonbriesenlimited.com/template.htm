Welcome to Habaneros Sports Bar and Grill

Looking for a place to eat some spicy food and watch the big game? Come to Habaneros where the food is hot and the beer is cold. Here we serve some of the best food in the South. We have everything like Jerk Chicken, Cajun Jamabalya, Grilled Steaks and Chops, Shrimp and Grits, Slow Smoked Babyback ribs, and of course some of the hottest wings on the planet. Although we serve seriously hot and spicy food, not all of it is hot , so its the perfect place for everyone.
